Recipe - Croque Madame with Canadian Bacon

  1. Prepare baking sheet with parchment paper or silpat liner, set aside. Preheat broiler.
  2. In medium sized skillet add butter and melt over medium heat.
    1. Once butter is melted, whisk in flour until mixture is smooth and there are no clumps.
    2. Slowly whisk in milk and whisk until smooth and mixture starts to thicken.
    3. Remove from heat and whisk in 1/2 cup swiss cheese, fresh thyme leaves, grated nutmeg, kosher salt and ground pepper.
  3. Remove from heat and whisk in 1/2 cup swiss cheese, fresh thyme leaves, grated nutmeg, kosher salt and ground pepper.
  4. Top 2 slices of bread with 4 slices Canadian Bacon and 2 tablespoons shredded swiss cheese.
  5. Place bread slices on baking sheet. Broil in oven until cheese is melted, browned and bubbly.
  6. Remove from oven and top with remaining slice of bread, cheese sauce side down.
    1. Top each sandwich with equal parts swiss cheese sauce and remaining shredded swiss cheese.
    2. Place back under broiler and cook until cheese is fully melted and bubbly.
  7. Enjoy food!
